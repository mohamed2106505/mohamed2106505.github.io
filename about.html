About Me

Hi, I’m Mohamed Hisham, a Junior Software Engineer based in Cairo, Egypt, with a strong passion for building impactful digital solutions.

I specialize in full-stack development and mobile applications, and I enjoy turning ideas into functional, user-friendly software.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Misr International University (MIU), where I specialized in Software Engineering. My hands-on experience spans various technologies — from Flutter, Spring Boot, and Node.js to AI-powered systems using FastAPI and Firebase.

Some of my key projects include:

  • HeartGuard — an AI-driven mobile app that monitors heart activity using sensors and real-time data analysis.
  • E-commerce platforms like Clothy and Jewelry Store Website, showcasing my ability to build complete web solutions.
  • Interactive desktop applications and games, such as Mine Eraser and Bank Management System.

I’m constantly exploring new tools and frameworks, with a focus on AI integration, system design, and scalable web solutions. My goal is to contribute to innovative projects that make technology more accessible and intelligent.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy learning about emerging technologies, refining UI/UX designs, and collaborating with creative teams to bring ideas to life.
